Who I Am

I'm a cybersecurity graduate and PJPT-certified penetration tester with a strong foundation in offensive security, Active Directory exploitation, and OSINT. I approach security with both an attacker's mindset and a builder's curiosity.

My graduation project, Hawk Eye, is a full OSINT platform for extracting intelligence from the dark web (.onion sites) and Telegram channels. It features async Tor-based crawling, WHOIS and DNS lookups, Reverse DNS analysis, and a SHA-512 password leak checker backed by SQLite — built entirely from scratch.

I qualified as a Black Hat MEA 2024 CTF finalist and completed intensive Cyber Warriors training at Jordan's National Cyber Security Center. Currently pursuing CHFI and CND while interning at SMT Academy.
